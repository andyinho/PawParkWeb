Popular Destinations

Wilshire Park

Wilshire Park is a 14.4-acre (5.8 ha) park in northeast Portland, Oregon. The park was acquired in 1940 and is maintained by Portland Parks & Recreation.

Fernhill Park

This 26.71-acre natural forest garden includes a wide variety of native plants, split-rail fencing, logs and boulders, and a gravel path.

Irving Park

Irving Park is a city park of about 16 acres (6.5 ha) in northeast Portland, Oregon. Park amenities include fields for baseball, soccer, and an off-leash area.

Mt. Tabor Park

The Mount Tabor Dog Park is, essentially, a small, old growth forest. While there is fencing, the off-leash area is only fenced on three sides.

Wallace Dog Park

Wallace Dog Park, one of Portland, Oregon's many parks with designated off-leash areas, is a beloved playground housed on 5.39 acres of property.

1000 Acre Park

Thousand Acres, is the off-leash mecca of Portland. With 1,400 acres of open field, wetlands, countless trails, and two rivers where your dog can splash around, and run free, this is a dog's paradise.
